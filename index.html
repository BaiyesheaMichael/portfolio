I have a deep passion for building secure, intelligent systems that not only solve problems but also drive meaningful impact. Earlier in my career, I contributed significantly to the development of Dev1Kit/, a powerful software framework that helps reduce development time by over 50 percent. The framework powers HYELLA, a health management system used in more than 18 hospitals across Nigeria, supporting the care of over 200,000 patients. I also served as the system architect on the Payment Management and Information System (PMIS) project, where I led a team in building the platform that facilitated more than 2 billion Naira in payments to local beneficiaries. These projects have been some of my proudest contributions to large-scale, real-world systems among others.


In 2024, I designed and built an automated API security testing tool from the ground up. The tool is capable of running comprehensive tests across multiple endpoints, simulating a variety of real-world threats including SQL injection, XSS, broken authentication, encryption issues, and data exposure. Building this tool was driven by my strong desire to solve complex challenges through hands-on engineering. I enjoy creating solutions that not only work efficiently but also enhance the resilience and security of systems in practical, meaningful ways.


I am a current professional member of the ISC2 and have hands-on experience across secure development, vulnerability testing, and architectural leadership. I bring a practical, thoughtful approach to engineering and a commitment to delivering systems that work reliably and securely in the real world.
